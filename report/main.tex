\begin{abstract}

A master's thesis investigated extending Kleenex with approximate matching by rewriting Core Kleenex. We design and perform experiments to evaluate the performance of this approximate Kleenex as well as three other tools for comparison. The experiment result shows it is not practical so far because of the limitation of long compiling time or the small number of error tolerance.  We present our analysis of this problem by ??. We also conduct a survey of approximate matching techniques, and at last discuss the possibilities of improving it. 
\end{abstract}
